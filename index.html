About

The Eye Behind the Lens

I'm Ashton, the photographer behind ALKY's Mind's Eye. Based in Singapore, I find beauty in the interplay between light and shadow — whether I'm exploring the vibrant streets of Chinatown, capturing intimate portraits at golden hour, or documenting my travels across Southeast Asia.

Every frame tells a story. My approach blends technical precision with an intuitive sense for the decisive moment. From street photography to creative portraiture, I bring a cinematic, moody aesthetic that transforms fleeting moments into lasting impressions.

Services

Travel & Landscape

Cinematic travel photography that captures the spirit of a place — from sweeping vistas to quiet, intimate details across Southeast Asia and beyond.

Street & Urban

Candid moments and city narratives. I document Singapore's urban life — Chinatown heritage, shophouse facades, rain-soaked streets, and everyday stories.

Portraits

Environmental and creative portraiture with natural light and moody tones. Personal branding, lifestyle shoots, and expressive character studies.

Events & Commercial

Professional coverage of corporate events, conferences, product launches, and brand experiences. Polished and publication-ready.
